Installing Knox
You can install Knox for Cloudera Cybersecurity Platform (CCP) with Ambari. The Knox
      service option is available through the Add Service wizard after CCP is installed.
Login to Ambari at http://$AMBARI_HOST:8080. 

In the left navigation menu, click Actions, then select
                  Add Service.

On the Add Service Wizard page, select
                  Knox, then click Next.

You are prompted to Assign Masters. Make a note of the Knox Gateway host(s) for use
               in subsequent installation steps. Click Next.

Enter a password in the Knox Master Secret field, then click
                  Next.

Confirm the Ambari recommended changes for your dependent configuration, then click
                  OK.

Click Deploy.
