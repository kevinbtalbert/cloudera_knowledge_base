Viewing queues and queue details
The Queues page is scheduler dependent and displays details of
    YARN queues. You can either view queues from all the partitions or filter to view queues of a
    partition.
Capacity Scheduler






View queue details
In the capacity scheduler view, click the circle that represents a particular queue. The
        right column of the page gets updated with details of that queue.
