Enabling an alert policy
You can use Streams Messaging Manager (SMM) to enable an alert policy in your
        environment.

Perform the following steps to enable an alert policy:


From the left navigation pane, select Alerts.
The Alerts Overview page appears.

Click ALERT POLICIES.

Find the alert policy that you want to enable, and click the enable icon beside
                    the alert policy.
The alert policy is enabled.
