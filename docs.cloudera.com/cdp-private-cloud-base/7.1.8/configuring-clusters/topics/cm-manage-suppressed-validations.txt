Managing Suppressed Validations


On pages where you have suppressed validations, you
        see a link that says Show
            #
          Suppressed Warning(s). On this screen, you can: 
Click the Show # Suppressed
              Warning(s) link to show the warnings.Each suppressed warning displays an icon:
                 .

Click the Unsuppress... link
            to unsuppress the configuration validation. 
Click the Hide Suppressed
              Warnings link to re-hide the suppressed warnings.
