Assign Roles
The Assign Roles page suggests role assignments for the hosts in
    your cluster. 
You can click on the hostname for a role to select a different host. You can also click the
        View By Host button to see all the roles assigned to a host.
After assigning all of the roles for your services, click Continue.
      The Setup Database page displays.
