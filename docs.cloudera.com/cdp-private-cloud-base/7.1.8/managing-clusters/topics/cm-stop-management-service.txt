Stopping the Cloudera Management Service
How to stop the Cloudera Management Service. 

Minimum Required Role:
Limited Cluster
            Administrator  (also provided by Full
          Administrator and Cluster
          Administrator)


Do one of the following:


Select Clusters > Cloudera Management Service.
Select
                    Actions > Stop.


On the
                Home > Status
            tab, click the options menu to the right of Cloudera Management
              Service and select Stop.

Click Stop to confirm. The Command Details window
          shows the progress of stopping the roles.

When Command completed with
          n/n successful subcommands
      appears, the task is complete. Click Close.
