Configuring Alerts Transitioning Out of Alerting Health Threshold
You can configure an alert when a service or role instance transitions from an
    alerting to a non-alerting health threshold.

Minimum Required Role:
Limited Cluster
            Administrator  (also provided by Full
          Administrator and Cluster
          Administrator)


Select
            Administration > Alerts.Click  to the right of Alert on Transitions out of Alerting
            Health.Select
                Scope > role_name or
                service_name
        (Service-Wide).In the category Event Server Default Group, check the
            Alert on Transitions out of Alerting Health
        checkbox.Enter a Reason for change, and then click Save
            Changes to commit the changes.Click the Cloudera Manager logo to return to the Home page.Click the 
          icon that is next to any stale services to invoke the cluster restart wizard.
