Configuring Service Monitoring
You can configure different aspects of service monitoring. After you configure
    monitoring for a service, restart the cluster.

Minimum Required Role:
Configurator (also provided by
          Cluster Administrator,
Limited Cluster Administrator , and
          Full Administrator)


Select
              Clusters > cluster_name > service_name > .
Click the Configuration tab.Select
                Scope > service_name
              (Service-Wide).Select
              Category > Monitoring.Locate the property to change or search for it by typing its name in the
            Search box.Configure the property.Enter a Reason for change, and then click Save
            Changes to commit the changes.Click the Cloudera Manager logo to return to the Home page.Click the 
          icon that is next to any stale services to invoke the cluster restart wizard.
