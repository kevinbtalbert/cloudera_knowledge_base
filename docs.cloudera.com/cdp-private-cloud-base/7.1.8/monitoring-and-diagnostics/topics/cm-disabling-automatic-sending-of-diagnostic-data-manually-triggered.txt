Disabling the Automatic Sending of Diagnostic Data from a Manually Triggered
    Collection
If you do not want data automatically sent to Cloudera after manually triggering data
    collection, you can disable this feature. The data you collect will be saved and can be
    downloaded for sending to Cloudera Support at a later time.Select
              Administration > Settings.Under the Support category, uncheck
        the box for Send Diagnostic Data to Cloudera
        Automatically.Enter a Reason for change, and then
          click Save Changes to commit the
        changes.
