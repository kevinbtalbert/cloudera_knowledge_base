Suppressing a Health Test
You can suppress a health test to hide its results.

Minimum Required Role:
Configurator (also provided by
          Cluster Administrator,
Limited Cluster Administrator , and
          Full Administrator)


Go to the health test you want to suppress.
Click the Suppress...
          link to the right of the health test description.A dialog box opens
          where you can enter a comment about the suppression action.Click Confirm.
          The display changes to Suppressing... while the change is propagated.
