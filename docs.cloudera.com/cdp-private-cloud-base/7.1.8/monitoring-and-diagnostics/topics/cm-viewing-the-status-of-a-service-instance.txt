Viewing the Status of a Service Instance
You can view the status of a service instance from the Status
    page or the Clusters menu.
Click the Cloudera Manager logo to go to the Home page.Access the service.
In the Status tab, select 
ClusterName
 > 
ServiceName
.
Select Clusters > 
ClusterName
 > 
ServiceName
.

The Status page opens. On the
        Status page you can view a variety of information about a service and
      its performance.
