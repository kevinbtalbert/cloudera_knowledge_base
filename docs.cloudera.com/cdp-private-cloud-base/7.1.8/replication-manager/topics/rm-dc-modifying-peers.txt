Modifying peers to use in replication policy
After you add a replication source as a peer, you can modify or delete the peers as
    required.
Go to the Cloudera Manager > Replication > Peers page. 

Select a peer, and click Actions > Edit.

Update the peer configuration as required, and click Update Peer
          to save your changes.

tipSelect a peer, and click Actions > Delete to delete the peer.
