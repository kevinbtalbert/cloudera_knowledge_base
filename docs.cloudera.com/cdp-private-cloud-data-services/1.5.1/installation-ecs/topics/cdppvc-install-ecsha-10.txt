Adding Role Instances to Containerised Cluster
You must add the role instances to the containerised cluster.

Log in to Cloudera Manager.
Navigate to the ECS service.
Click the Actions drop-down.
Click the Add Role Instances button. The Add Role
     Instances page appears.
In the Assign Roles page, specify the role assignments for your new
    roles. Click Continue.
In the Review Changes page, click Finish.
