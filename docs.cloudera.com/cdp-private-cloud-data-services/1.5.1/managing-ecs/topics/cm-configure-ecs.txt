Configuring the Embedded Container Service

You use Cloudera Manager to configure the Embedded Container Service and
    clusters.
Open the Cloudera Manager Admin ConsoleFrom the Home page, Click on the Embedded Container Service
          ClusterClick the Hosts, ECS service, or the
            Docker service links.Click the Configuration tab.Use the Filters or Search functions to locate the
          configuration property you are looking for.Enter your change.Click Save Changes.
