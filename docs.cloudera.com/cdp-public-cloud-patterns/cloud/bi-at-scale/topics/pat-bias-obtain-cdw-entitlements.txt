Activating features under entitlement
Unified Analytics and Data Visualization features in Cloudera Data Warehouse (CDW)
        are under entitlement. Contact your Cloudera Account Representative to activate these
        features for your environment.

Activate the following entitlements after you register your cloud environment
                    with CDP:


CDW_STANDALONE_DATA_VISUALIZATION: To enable Cloudera
                            Data Visualization
CDW_FENG_IMPALA: To use Unified Analytics in Hue
