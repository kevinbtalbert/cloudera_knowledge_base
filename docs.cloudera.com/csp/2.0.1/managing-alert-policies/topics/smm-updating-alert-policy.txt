Updating an Alert Policy
You can use SMM to update an alert policy in your environment.
Perform the following steps to update an alert policy:

From the left navigation pane, select Alerts.
The Alerts Overview page appears.

Click ALERT POLICIES.

Find the alert policy that you want to update, and click the pencil icon beside
                    the alert policy.
The Alert Policy window appears.

Edit the properties.

Click Save.
