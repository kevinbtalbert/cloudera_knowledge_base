Interacting with a Session in Cloudera Data Engineering
Once a Session is created in Cloudera Data Engineering (CDE), you can begin your
    interaction. 
Ensure that you are using a version of CDE 1.19 or higher for your Virtual
      Cluster.

In the Cloudera Data Platform (CDP) console, click the Data
            Engineering tile. The Home page displays.

Click Sessions in the left navigation menu.

Click the Interact tab.

In the Input box, enter your commands.

Click Run. A history of executed commands displays in the
          sequence of their execution.
