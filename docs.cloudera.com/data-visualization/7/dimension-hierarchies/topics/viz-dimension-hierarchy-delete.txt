Deleting dimension hierarchies


To delete the hierarchy, you must make changes in the dataset Fields
        interface.


Click Edit Fields.

In the list of Dimensions, find the hierarchy to delete, and
          click the Pencil icon next to it.

We are deleting the Region hierarchy.





In the Edit Hierarchy modal window, click Delete
            Hierarchy.






Click Save.

The hierarchy no longer appears in the list of dimensions.
