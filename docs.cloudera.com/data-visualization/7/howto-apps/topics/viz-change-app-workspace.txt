Changing application workspace
CDP Data Visualization enables you to create an app in the Private workspace and then
    change the assigned workspace before sharing it.
Navigate to the app.In the top navigation bar of App Designer, click
          PRIVATE.

In the drop-down menu, select Public, and then click
            Move.

After a brief success message, the image appears on the screen, and the app is now in
            the PUBLIC workspace.
