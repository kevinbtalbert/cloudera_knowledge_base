Rearranging tabs
In the App Designer of CDP Data Visualization, you can easily change the order of tabs
    through drag and drop interaction.

In the App Navigation interface, click to select the tab that
          you plan to move. We chose the Maps tab.

Drag the tab to the first position, and drop it.

Click Save.


To familiarize yourself with app customization, see Customizing
        applications.
To learn how to launch the app you created, see Launching applications.
