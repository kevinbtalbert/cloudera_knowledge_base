Adding custom styles to apps
CDP Data Visualization enables you to add custom styling to applications.
To use the Custom Style menu, follow
      these steps.
 On the right side of App Designer, click the
            Style menu.






In the Style menu, click Custom
          Style.





Select one of the following options.
