Changing application style
The Style menu contains a large selection of options for
    customizing the appearance of apps. To use the  Style menu, follow these
    steps. On the right side of App Designer, click the
            Style menu.  




 In the Style menu, review the additional menus and select the
          relevant option according to your needs.
