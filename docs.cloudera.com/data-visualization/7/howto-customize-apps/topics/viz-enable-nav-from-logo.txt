Enabling navigation from logoCloudera DocsEnabling navigation from logo
The Settings menu contains the general options for customizing
    the behavior of apps. You can use it to provide users access to the
      Visuals page by clicking the logo in the menu bar of the
    navigation.
On the right side of the App Designer, click the
            Settings (cogwheel) menu.





In the Settings menu, click
          General.
Select the following option: Enable navigation to the Visuals page on click
            of the logo in the menu bar.

By default, this option is not enabled.
