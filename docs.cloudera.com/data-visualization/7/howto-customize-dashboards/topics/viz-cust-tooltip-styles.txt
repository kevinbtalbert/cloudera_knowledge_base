Tooltips in dashboards


You can adjust several style options for tooltips in CDP Data Visualization dashboards.
To get to the options in the Visual Axes and Labels menu, follow
        these steps:


On the right side of Dashboard Designer, click the
            Style menu.

In the Style menu, click Tooltips.

Select one of the options.
