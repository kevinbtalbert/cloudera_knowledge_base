Adding custom styles to dashboards 
Cloudera Data Visualization supports several options for custom styling on
    dashboards.
To change the options for custom styles on dashboards, follow
      these steps:

 On the right side of Dashboard Designer, click the
            Custom Style menu.

In the Style menu, choose from the available custom style
          options.
