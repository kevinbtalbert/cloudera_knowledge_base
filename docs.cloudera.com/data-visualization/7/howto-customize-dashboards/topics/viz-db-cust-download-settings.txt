Customizing download settings for dashboards 
Cloudera Data Visualization supports several options for dashboard download settings.
        To change the options, follow these steps. 
 On the right side of Dashboard Designer, click the
                        Settings menu.

In the SETTINGS menu, click
                        Downloads to open the download options.
Select the option you need.
