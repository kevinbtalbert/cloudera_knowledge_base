Customizing general settings for dashboards 
CDP Data Visualization supports several options for display settings for dashboards. To
    change the options for dashboard settings, follow these steps.  On the right side of Visual Designer, click the
            Settings menu.In the Settings menu, click General
          to open the menu.Select the option you need.
