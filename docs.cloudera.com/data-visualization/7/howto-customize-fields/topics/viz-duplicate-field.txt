Duplicating an item
 In CDP Data Visualization you can duplicate an item on the shelf, and then re-use it
    for another calculation or for placement on another shelf of the visual.
To duplicate a field, follow these steps.Click the field you plan to modify, to open the Field
            Properties menu.Under Field Properties, click
            Duplicate.



Depending on the requirements of your work, edit the field, and/or move it to
          another shelf.
