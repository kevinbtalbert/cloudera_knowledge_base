Changing font weight in the aggregate row of a visual

This applies to Cross Tabulation
      visuals.
On the right side of Visual Designer, click the
            Style menu.


In the Settings menu, click Aggregate Row
            Style..


To change the font weight used by the aggregate row of a visual, navigate to the
            Style menu, and select Font Weight from
          the Aggregate Row Style menu and choose a fonnt weight.
