Changing font style in the aggregate row of a visual

This applies to Cross Tabulation
      visuals.
On the right side of Visual Designer, click the
            Style menu.


In the Settings menu, click Aggregate Row
            Style..


To change the font used by the aggregate row of a visual, select Font
            Style from the Aggregate Row Style menu and choose a
          font style.
