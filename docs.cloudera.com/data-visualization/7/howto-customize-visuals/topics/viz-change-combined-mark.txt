Changing combined node mark


On the right side of Visual Designer, click
            Settings.
In the Settings menu, click
          Nodes.

To change the mark that applies to both the source and target node, change the
          selection in the Combined Node Mark menu.
These options are:

circle (default)
square
diamond
cross
triangle-up
triangle-down
