Changing link strengthCloudera DocsChanging link strength


To change the strength of a link, navigate to the Links menu, and
        change the minimum and the maximum in the Link strength option. The
        default setting is 0.1, and the valid range is 0 to
          1.



Compare the appearance of a visuals with link strength of 0.01,
          0.1, and 1.
