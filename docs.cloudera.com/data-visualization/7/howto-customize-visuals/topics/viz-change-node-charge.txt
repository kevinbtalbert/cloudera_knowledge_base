Changing node chargeCloudera DocsChanging node charge


On the right side of Visual Designer, click
            Settings.
In the Settings menu, click
          Nodes.

To change the charge of a node, adjust the selector for the Node
            charge option.The default setting is -1000. The valid range is -3000 to
            0.

Compare the appearance of visuals with node charge of -100,
              -300, and -500.
