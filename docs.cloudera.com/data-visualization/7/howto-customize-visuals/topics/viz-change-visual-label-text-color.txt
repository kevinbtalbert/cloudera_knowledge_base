Label text color in a visual

On the right side of Visual Designer, click the
            Settings menu.


In the Settings menu, click Visual
            Title.

To change the color used by the legend of a visual, navigate to the
              Style > Visual Axes and
            Labels menu, and make adjustments in the Label Text
            Color selector.

        scheme in labels, and the second one uses black font color.
