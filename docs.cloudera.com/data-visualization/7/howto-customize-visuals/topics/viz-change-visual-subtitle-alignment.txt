Changing text alignment in the subtitle of a visual

On the right side of Visual Designer, click the
            Settings menu.


In the Settings menu, click Visual
            Subtitle.


To change the text alignment of the subtitle of a visual, navigate to the
            Visual Subtitle menu, and change the selection of the
            Alignment option. Text is aligned left by default, and has the
          additional options for center and right text alignment.
