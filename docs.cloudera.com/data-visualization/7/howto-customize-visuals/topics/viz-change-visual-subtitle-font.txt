Changing font family in the subtitle of a visual 

On the right side of Visual Designer, click the
            Settings menu.


In the Settings menu, click Visual
            Title.

To change the font used by the subtitle of a visual, navigate to the
            Visual Subtitle menu, open the Font Family
          menu, and make a selection.
