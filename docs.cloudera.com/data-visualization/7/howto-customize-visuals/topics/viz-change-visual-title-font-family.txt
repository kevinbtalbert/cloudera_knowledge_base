Changing font family in the title of a visual

On the right side of Visual Designer, click the
            Settings menu.


In the Settings menu, click Visual
            Title.


To change the font used by the title of a visual, navigate to the Visual
            Titlemenu, and make a selection from the Font Family
          menu.
