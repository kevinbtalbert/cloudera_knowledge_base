Changing font weight in the title of a visual

On the right side of Visual Designer, click the
            Settings menu.


In the Settings menu, click Visual
            Title.


To change the font weight used by the title of a visual, navigate to the
            Visual Title menu,  open the Font Weight
          menu, and make a selection.
