Changing font size in the title of a visual 

On the right side of Visual Designer, click the
            Settings menu.


In the Settings menu, click Visual
            Title.


To change the font size used by the title of a visual, navigate to the
            Visual Title menu, and make adjustments in the Font
            Size selector.
