Displaying legend in choropleth map
In an Interactive Map visual, CDP Data Visualization enables you to display a
    legend in a choropleth map.

On the right side of Visual Designer, click
            Settings.
In the Settings menu, click
            Choropleth.


To display a legend, select the Include legend option.



In the following image, notice the legend at the bottom right corner, with gradient of
            colors showing the average tax liability in California by zip code.
