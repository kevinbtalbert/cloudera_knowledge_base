Displaying circles
 In an interactive map visual, CDP Data Visualization enables you to display
    circles.

On the right side of Visual Designer, click
            Settings.
In the Settings menu, click
          Circles.



To show or hide Circles, select or unselect the Enable Circle option.
          This option is off by default.
