Displaying gauge line on qualitative arc


In a Gauge visual, CDP Data Visualization enables you to compare the measure value to the
        maximum qualitative range.
To show a compare line on the qualitative arc, navigate to the Marks
        menu, and select the Display compare line on qualitative arc
        option.





In the second image, note the presence of a gauge line on the qualitative arc.
