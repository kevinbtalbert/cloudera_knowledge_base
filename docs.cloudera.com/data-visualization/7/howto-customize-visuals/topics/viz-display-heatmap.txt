Displaying heatmap

In an Interactive Map visual, CDP Data Visualization enables
      you to show or hide the Heatmap.

On the right side of Visual Designer, click
            Settings.
In the Settings menu, click
          Heatmap.


To show or hide the Heatmap, select or unselect Enable
            Heatmap option. This option is on by default.

If you disable the Heatmap option, Google map renders only with the Cluster option, the
            other default.



Similarly, the Mapbox map renders without the Heatmap option.
