Enabling routes
In an interactive visual, you can choose show and hide routes.

On the right side of Visual Designer, click
            Settings.
In the Settings menu, click
          Circles.




In an Interactive Map visual, to show or hide Routes, select or
          unselect the Enable Routes option. 

This option is off by default.
