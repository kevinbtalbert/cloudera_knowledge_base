Enabling reverse color palette of visuals

Cloudera Data Visualization enables you to reverse the color
      palette in your visualizations.
To reverse the color palette in your visualization, select Reverse order
            of colors palettes.



Note that the colors in the visual flip; the legend is a good indicator of this.

The color reverse effect is particularly noticeable on monochromatic and dichromatic
            color palettes, which typically use a gradient legend.
