Showing expansion totals above expansion rows


In cross tabulation visuals, the subtotals and combined total appear under the detailed
        data, by default. To display subtotals and combined total over the expanded rows, navigate
        to the Marks menu, and select Show expansion totals above
          the expanded rows.



Before selecting this option, notice that the subtotals and combined total appear at the
        bottom of expanded rows.



After selecting this option, notice that the subtotals and combined total appear over the
        expanded rows.
