Cloning email templates


To make a clone from an existing email template, follow these steps:


Click the Gear icon on the main navigation bar to open the
            Administration menu.

Select Email Templates.






In the Manage Email Templates interface, click the
            Pencil icon to edit the template.






Click CLONE.






In the cloned template, make the necessary changes and click
            SAVE.






Click the Email Templates  tab at the top of the screen to see
          the updated item in the list of templates.
