Deleting email templates


To delete an existing email template, follow these steps.


Click the Gear icon on the main navigation bar to open the
            Administration menu.

Select Email Templates.






In the Manage Email Templates interface, click the
            Trashcan icon to delete the template.






In the Delete Confirmation modal window, type DELETE, and
          then click DELETE.







A success message is displayed briefly and the list of templates no longer includes the
        deleted one.
