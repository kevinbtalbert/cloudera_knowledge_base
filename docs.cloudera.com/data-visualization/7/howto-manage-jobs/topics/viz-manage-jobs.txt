Managing jobs
Cloudera Data Visualization enables you to manage jobs, scheduled jobs, and schedule
    intervals.

noteUsers without administrative privileges can only edit jobs created by them.


Click the Gear icon on the main navigation bar to open the
            Administration menu.

Click Job Status to get to the Jobs
          interface.







The Jobs interface appears, which has three main tabs:

Job Logs
Scheduled Jobs
Manage Schedule Intervals
