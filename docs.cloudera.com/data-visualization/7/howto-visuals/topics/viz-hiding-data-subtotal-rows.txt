Hiding data and subtotal rows
To hide the expanded rows and subtotals, follow these steps.

Click the - (minus) icon in the year and
                        state columns.






The expanded rows and subtotals no longer appear in the visual. Note that a
                        (plus)  icon appears again in the year and
                        state columns.
