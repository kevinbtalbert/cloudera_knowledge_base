Cloudera Data Visualization






Release Notes





Cloudera Data Visualization is a dashboarding and application building service that enables you to explore data
through visuals. You can analyze your data, collaborate with team members and staekholders, and communicate data insights
across teams without coding or moving data to third party apps.
This content is designed for business analysts, data analysts, data scientists, line-of-business users, and other
data enthusiasts. You can learn how to deploy Data Visualization on the Cloudera Data Platform (CDP), how to connect Data Visualization
to your data sets, how to use the various visualization features, or how to create interactive applications and share them with a wider audience.
