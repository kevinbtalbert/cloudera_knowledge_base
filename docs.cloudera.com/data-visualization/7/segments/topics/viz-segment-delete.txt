Deleting segments
CDP Data Visualization lets you easily delete dataset segment definition.

On the main navigation bar, click DATA.

Select Flight Delays dataset.Dataset side navigation appears, open
          at Dataset Detail view.
In the side navigation menu, click Segments.

In the Segments list, find the segment to delete, and click
            Delete.

In this example, we are deleting Short Flights. 





Click Delete in the confirmation modal window.



The segment Short Flights no longer appears in the
              Segments interface.
