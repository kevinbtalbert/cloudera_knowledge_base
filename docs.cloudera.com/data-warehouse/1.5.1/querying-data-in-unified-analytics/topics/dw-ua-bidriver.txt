Get the JDBC driver
To query, analyze, and visualize data stored in CDW Unified Analytics, you must
        download the Unified Analytics JDBC driver to your cluster and provide it to your client.
        You can use the latest version of drivers provided by Cloudera to connect Unified Analytics
        to Business Intelligence (BI) tools.
