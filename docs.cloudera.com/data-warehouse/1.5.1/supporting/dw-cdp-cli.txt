CDP CLI for Data Warehouse
CDP CLI allows you to manage users, environments and other entities in your Private
  Cloud deployment. After you setup and configure CDP CLI on your clusters, you can use the various
  commands and sub-commands that are available.
Commands for Cloudera Data Warehouse are available here. To see whether a command or a sub-command is available for Private
   Cloud, check the "Form Factor" section.
