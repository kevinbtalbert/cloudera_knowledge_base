Inspecting dashboard data and queries
 You can drill down into details of resource usage of Hive or Impala, get statistics
        about usage, and run queries on the details.

Open a dashboard, for example the HiveServer2 dashboard, and click the title in
                    a row, for example, the Heap usage title.






Click Inspect > Data.
The total max heap usage over time appears.




Click Stats or Query to view
                    statistics or queries.

In the HiveServer2 dashboard, and click the Heap usage title > More > Duplicate panel content.






In the HiveServer2 dashboard, and click the Heap usage title > More > Copy panel content to another dashboard.
