Deprecated Items

A deprecated item is a feature, component, platform, or functionality that Cloudera is planning to remove in a future release. Cloudera supports items that are deprecated until they are
removed, and the deprecation gives customers time to plan for removal.

Apache Crunch is deprecated, and will be removed in a future release. Cloudera recommends using Spark 2 instead. Additionally, as of
