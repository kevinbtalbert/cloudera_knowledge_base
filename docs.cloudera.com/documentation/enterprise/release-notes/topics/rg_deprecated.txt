Terminology

Deprecated
Feature, component, platform, or functionality that Cloudera is planning to remove in a future release. Cloudera supports items that are deprecated until they are removed, and the
deprecation gives customers time to plan for removal.
Removed
Feature, component, platform, or functionality that has been removed from the product and is no longer supported.
