Impala Version and Download Information

The information in this section applies to CDH 4 clusters, where Impala is downloaded and installed separately from CDH itself. You can disregard this section if you are running a CDH 5
cluster. This information is included with the CDH 5 documentation for users who manage CDH 4 clusters through Cloudera Manager 5.
