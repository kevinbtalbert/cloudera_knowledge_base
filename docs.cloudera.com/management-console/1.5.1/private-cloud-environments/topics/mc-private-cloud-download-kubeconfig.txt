    only)
On OpenShift deployments, you can download and view the kubeconfig file used to register
    your environment.Navigate to the Management Console > Environments.Click the title of your environment.Click the Compute Cluster tab.
Click Actions > Download Kubernetes
            Configuration. 
The Kubernetes configuration file is downloaded to your system.
