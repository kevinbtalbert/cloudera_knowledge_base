Changing your Local Administrator password
Management Console uses a default password for your Local Administrator account. After
    you access Management Console for the first time you should change your Local Administrator
    password for security reasons.


Sign in to the CDP console. 

Click Management Console.

On the Management Console home page, select
            Administration>Authentication.

Click Change Password  then enter and confirm your new
          password.

Click Change to save your changes.
