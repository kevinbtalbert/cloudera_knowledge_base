Metrics and Insight
You can view the quotas and metrics about the quotas in the HBase Master user
        interface. The list of defined quotas are displayed along with those quotas whose violation
        policy is being enforced.The list of tables/namespaces with enforced violation policies is also available in the the JMX
            metrics exposed by the Master.
