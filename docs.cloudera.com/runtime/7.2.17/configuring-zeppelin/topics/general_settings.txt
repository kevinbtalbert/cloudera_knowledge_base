General Settings
This section provides additional information about Shiro general
    settings. 



securityManager.sessionManager.globalSessionTimeout


specifies how long to wait (in milliseconds) before logging out a user, if they
                     are logged in and are not moving the cursor.
The default is 86,400,000 milliseconds, which equals 24 hours.
