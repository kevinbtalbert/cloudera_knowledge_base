Deleting an alert policy
You can use Streams Messaging Manager (SMM) to delete an alert policy in your
        environment.
Perform the following steps to delete an alert policy:

From the left navigation pane, select Alerts.
The Alerts Overview page appears.

Click ALERT POLICIES.

Find the alert policy that you want to delete, and click the delete icon beside
                    the alert policy.

Click Yes.
