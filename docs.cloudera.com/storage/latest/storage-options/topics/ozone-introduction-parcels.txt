Introduction to Ozone Parcels
This document helps you to install and manage Ozone parcels on top of the CDP clusters. 
Ozone parcel releases enable you to upgrade the Ozone software at a cadence higher than LTS
      and feature releases of CDP. This enables delivering the upstream innovation in the open
      source version of Ozone sooner to customers.
