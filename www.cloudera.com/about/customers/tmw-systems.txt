TMW Systems develops enterprise management software for the transportation services industry, including logistics, freight, trucking and heavy-duty repair and maintenance. The company has several thousand customers that rely on its ERP system to manage up to four million trucks daily.

TMW is now the leading Big Data transportation technology provider and uses Hortonworks to develop business intelligence and Big Data tools that offer valuable business insights to transportation application users. This management includes integrated dispatch, fleet maintenance, fuel optimization, and last mile routing in both cities and on highways. TMW’s specialized product and business intelligence optimization is used by many major corporations and Big 5 companies today.
