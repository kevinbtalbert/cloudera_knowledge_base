Cloudera Announces Speakers for Hadoop World: NYC 2009






 









Agenda Includes Hadoop Experts From Visa, JPMorgan Chase, New York Times, Facebook, Microsoft, Yahoo!, and More
SAN FRANCISCO, CA–(Marketwire – September 9, 2009) – Cloudera, the commercial Hadoop™ company, today announced the speakers for the inaugural Hadoop World conference, which will take place Friday, October 2 at the Roosevelt Hotel in New York City.
More than 30 of the world’s most experienced Hadoop practitioners from enterprise and academia — including project creator Doug Cutting — will gather to discuss Hadoop-related best practices and use cases. As the only Hadoop-centric event on the east coast, Hadoop World is designed for enterprises considering Hadoop while also catering to experienced users looking for more intensive instruction.
“We couldn’t have hoped for a better lineup at Hadoop World in New York City,” said Christophe Bisciglia, founder of Cloudera. “Attendees will hear firsthand from Hadoop experts within traditional enterprises how Hadoop is solving real business problems and modernizing their data management strategies.”
A partial list of confirmed speakers and their topics for discussion include:
– Joe Cunningham, Head of Technology Strategy and Innovation (VISA) -
Large Scale Transaction Analysis
– Rod Smith, Vice President (IBM) – Enabling ad-hoc Analytics at Web
Scale
– Eric Baldeschwieler, Vice President Hadoop Development (Yahoo!) -
Hadoop Applications at Yahoo!
– Ashish Thusoo, Software Engineer (Facebook) – Rethinking the Data
Warehouse with Hadoop and Hive
– Derek Gottfrid, Software Engineer (New York Times) – Counting
Clustering and other Data Tricks
– Stu Hood, Software Engineer (Rackspace) – Cross Data Center Logs
Analysis
– Aaron Cordova, Senior Consultant (Booz Allen) – Protein Alignment
– Bradford Stephens, Software Engineer (Visible Technologies) – A Real-
time Business Intelligence Platform
– Feng Cao, Deputy Director of Network, (China Mobile Research
Institute) – A Data Mining Platform for the Telecom Industry
For three days leading up to Hadoop World, Cloudera will offer training and certification for developers and administrators. For information (including a complete list of speakers) or to register for the conference, visit: Hadoop World: NYC 2009.
Hadoop World: NYC 2009 sponsors include: Yahoo!, Amazon.com, IBM, Intel, Facebook, Rackspace, Supermicro, Softlayer, and eHarmony.

About Cloudera
Cloudera is revolutionizing enterprise data management by offering the first unified Platform for big data, an enterprise data hub built on Apache Hadoop. Cloudera offers enterprises one place to store, access, process, secure, and analyze all their data, empowering them to extend the value of existing investments while enabling fundamental new ways to derive value from their data. Cloudera's open source big data platform is the most widely adopted in the world, and Cloudera is the most prolific contributor to the open source Hadoop ecosystem. As the leading educator of Hadoop professionals, Cloudera has trained over 40,000 individuals worldwide. Over 1,700 partners and a seasoned professional services team help deliver greater time to value. Leading organizations in every industry plus top public sector organizations globally run Cloudera in production.
