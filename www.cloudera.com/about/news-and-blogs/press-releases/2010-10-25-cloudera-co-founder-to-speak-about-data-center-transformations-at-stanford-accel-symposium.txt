Cloudera Co-Founder to Speak About Data Center Transformations at Stanford Accel Symposium






 









Jeff Hammerbacher, Co-founder and Vice President of Products at Cloudera, to Present on How the Cloud is Transforming the Entrepreneurial Landscape
Palo Alto, Calif. – Oct. 25, 2010 –
What: The Delta Conference: The 16th Stanford Accel Symposium on Digital Media and Technology
When: Oct. 27, 2010 from 9:15 – 10:30 a.m. PT
Where: Frances C. Arrillaga Alumni Center on the Stanford Campus
Jeff Hammerbacher, co-founder and vice president of products at Cloudera, is participating in a panel discussion titled, “Data Center Transformations,” that will put a spotlight on what database technologies are thriving and what the future holds for these solutions. This discussion will be held on Wednesday, Oct. 27, at the Stanford Accel Symposium on Digital Media and Technology.
The one-day show will focus on discussing the changing nature of media, technology and investing. More specifically, panelists will be challenged to share what sets apart the recent technology champions, who have been able to bear through this tumultuous climate. Hammerbacher will be speaking alongside executives from companies including Yahoo!, Facebook, Zynga, Nimbula, Membase and Fusion-io.
Hammerbacher has extensive experience working in the field of data analytics. Prior to Cloudera, he was an Entrepreneur in Residence at Accel Partners. Before Accel, he conceived, built and led the data team at Facebook where his team was responsible for driving Apache project Hive, a data warehouse infrastructure built on top of Apache Hadoop and Cassandra, a distributed storage system designed to scale across commodity servers.
For more information about the Stanford Accel Symposium or to register for the event, please visit https://www.accel.com/symposium/index.html.

About Cloudera
Cloudera is revolutionizing enterprise data management by offering the first unified Platform for big data, an enterprise data hub built on Apache Hadoop. Cloudera offers enterprises one place to store, access, process, secure, and analyze all their data, empowering them to extend the value of existing investments while enabling fundamental new ways to derive value from their data. Cloudera's open source big data platform is the most widely adopted in the world, and Cloudera is the most prolific contributor to the open source Hadoop ecosystem. As the leading educator of Hadoop professionals, Cloudera has trained over 40,000 individuals worldwide. Over 1,700 partners and a seasoned professional services team help deliver greater time to value. Leading organizations in every industry plus top public sector organizations globally run Cloudera in production.
