Cloudera Cofounder and CTO Amr Awadallah To Present at 2011 O'Reilly Strata Conference in New York City






 









Company to Exhibit at Strata Summit and Strata Conference, September 20-23
Palo Alto, CA – September 20, 2011 – Cloudera Inc., the leading provider of Apache Hadoop-based data management software and services, today announced that Cofounder and CTO Amr Awadallah, will be a featured speaker at O’Reilly’s Strata Conference, presenting “How Hadoop is Revolutionizing Business Intelligence and Advanced Data Analytics” in New York City on September 22, 2011. Awadallah, who oversees Cloudera’s contributions to the Apache Hadoop and other open source projects, as well as the engineering efforts for its commercial management software suite, Cloudera Enterprise, will discuss the evolution of business data architecture and how the advanced analytics capabilities of Apache Hadoop solve key business challenges in today’s enterprise.

What:
“How Hadoop is Revolutionizing Business Intelligence and Advanced Data Analytics”
Who:
Cloudera Cofounder and CTO Amr Awadallah
When:
Thursday, September 22, 2011 at 4:10pm ET
Where:
The O’Reilly Strata Conference: Making Data Work

New York Hilton

1335 Avenue of the Americas

New York, New York, 10019

Cloudera will be exhibiting at the Strata Summit September 20 – 21 and the Strata Conference, September 22 – 23, 2011. To learn more about Cloudera and its Apache Hadoop-based data management software and services, please stop by the company’s kiosk at either location.
To schedule a meeting with a Cloudera executive at either show, please contact Hope Nicora at 510-984-1527.
For more information about the O’Reilly Strata Summit and Conference, or to register to attend, visit: http://strataconf.com/stratany2011/

About Cloudera
Cloudera is revolutionizing enterprise data management by offering the first unified Platform for big data, an enterprise data hub built on Apache Hadoop. Cloudera offers enterprises one place to store, access, process, secure, and analyze all their data, empowering them to extend the value of existing investments while enabling fundamental new ways to derive value from their data. Cloudera's open source big data platform is the most widely adopted in the world, and Cloudera is the most prolific contributor to the open source Hadoop ecosystem. As the leading educator of Hadoop professionals, Cloudera has trained over 40,000 individuals worldwide. Over 1,700 partners and a seasoned professional services team help deliver greater time to value. Leading organizations in every industry plus top public sector organizations globally run Cloudera in production.
