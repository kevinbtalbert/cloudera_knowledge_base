Cloudera Software Engineer Todd Lipcon Presenting at the Fourth Annual Hadoop Conference Japan






 









Todd Lipcon to present an “Introduction to Apache Hadoop and CDH” and “Introduction to Apache HBase” for Japan’s Largest Hadoop User Group in Tokyo on September 26, 2011
Palo Alto, CA – September 26, 2011 – Cloudera Inc., the leading provider ofApache Hadoop-based data management software and services, announced today that Todd Lipcon (@tlipcon), Cloudera software engineer, will be presenting at the Fourth Annual Hadoop Conference in Tokyo Japan. Lipcon will be presenting two sessions at the conference: an “Introduction to Apache Hadoop and CDH” and “Introduction to Apache HBase.” Lipcon, who is a significant contributor to Apache Hadoop and other Hadoop projects such as Apache Thrift and HBase, will discuss the power of Hadoop and the larger ecosystem of open source projects built on and around the Hadoop core: Apache Hive, Pig, Flume, Oozie, HBase, Avro and more. Lipcon will also coverCDH, Cloudera’s entirely open source distribution of Apache Hadoop and explain why it is the easiest and most popular way to deploy Hadoop in critical enterprise environments worldwide.
The Annual Hadoop Conference in Japan is presented by the Hadoop User Group Japan. Over 800 Hadoop practitioners, developers and business executives will attend the conference in Tokyo on Monday, September 26, 2011 at the Bellesalle Shiodome Center.

Sessions: “Introduction to Apache Hadoop and CDH”

“Introduction to Apache HBase”

Cloudera Software Engineer Todd Lipcon

About Cloudera
Cloudera is revolutionizing enterprise data management by offering the first unified Platform for big data, an enterprise data hub built on Apache Hadoop. Cloudera offers enterprises one place to store, access, process, secure, and analyze all their data, empowering them to extend the value of existing investments while enabling fundamental new ways to derive value from their data. Cloudera's open source big data platform is the most widely adopted in the world, and Cloudera is the most prolific contributor to the open source Hadoop ecosystem. As the leading educator of Hadoop professionals, Cloudera has trained over 40,000 individuals worldwide. Over 1,700 partners and a seasoned professional services team help deliver greater time to value. Leading organizations in every industry plus top public sector organizations globally run Cloudera in production.
