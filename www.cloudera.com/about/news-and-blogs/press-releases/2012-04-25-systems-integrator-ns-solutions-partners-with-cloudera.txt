Systems Integrator NS Solutions Partners with Cloudera for Big Data Push in Japan






 









NS Solutions Becomes First Authorized Cloudera Reseller in Asia Pacific Market
PALO ALTO, CA AND TOKYO, JAPAN – April 25, 2012 – Cloudera, the leading provider of Apache Hadoop-based data management software, services and training, and NS Solutions, a leading systems integrator in Japan, announced today that they are partnering to provide Cloudera’s products to the Japanese market. This partnership will significantly expand the ability of Japanese companies to leverage Big Data by reducing the technical barriers to entry to using Apache Hadoop in production.
“We are pleased to establish a new partnership with Cloudera as the first Authorized Cloudera Reseller in the Asia Pacific market,” said Yutaka Miyabe, Senior Executive Director of NS Solutions. “We believe that the use of sophisticated Big Data technology is a critical element for the growth and innovation of Japanese companies.”
As an authorized Cloudera reseller, NS Solutions will provide its customers with end-to-end Hadoop solutions and services and will have access to Cloudera’s technical and sales resources to extend its solutions portfolio to meet customer demand for scalable enterprise-ready Apache Hadoop management tools and services. NS Solutions will be distributing and implementing Cloudera’s Distribution Including Apache Hadoop (CDH) and Cloudera Enterprise.
“We are very pleased to partner with NS Solutions in Japan,” said Tim Stevens, Vice President of Business and Corporate Development of Cloudera. “Japan is a strategic market for Cloudera’s global expansion, and this partnership will provide increased support and availability of services for Apache Hadoop users in the Asia Pacific region. NS Solutions’ extensive experience in data-centric implementations and services will contribute to Cloudera securing a firm foothold in this important market, while also speeding Japanese adoption of Apache Hadoop-based solutions.”
About NS Solutions
NS Solutions is a leading information technology solution provider in Japan. NS Solutions provides integrated solutions which address customers’ needs for both business applications and system platforms. The company provides end-to end services, including system consulting, planning, design, development, operation and maintenance and total end-to-end outsourcing services. NS Solutions is adept at providing multi-vendor and mission critical systems solutions, as well as incorporating best-of-breed products and technologies to address customers’ needs.

About Cloudera
Cloudera is revolutionizing enterprise data management by offering the first unified Platform for big data, an enterprise data hub built on Apache Hadoop. Cloudera offers enterprises one place to store, access, process, secure, and analyze all their data, empowering them to extend the value of existing investments while enabling fundamental new ways to derive value from their data. Cloudera's open source big data platform is the most widely adopted in the world, and Cloudera is the most prolific contributor to the open source Hadoop ecosystem. As the leading educator of Hadoop professionals, Cloudera has trained over 40,000 individuals worldwide. Over 1,700 partners and a seasoned professional services team help deliver greater time to value. Leading organizations in every industry plus top public sector organizations globally run Cloudera in production.
