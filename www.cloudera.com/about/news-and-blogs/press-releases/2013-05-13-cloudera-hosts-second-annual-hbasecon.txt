Cloudera Hosts Second Annual HBaseCon






 









Premier Apache HBase Community Event Brings Together Leading HBase Developers, Admins and Users

PALO ALTO, CA--(Marketwired - May 13, 2013) -

What:
Cloudera, the category leader that sets the standard for Apache Hadoop in the enterprise, will be hosting the second annual HBaseCon, the premier conference for the Apache HBase community. HBaseCon 2013 brings developers, contributors, users and administrators together offering them unparalleled educational and networking opportunities with the best and brightest in the community.
HBase is an open source, distributed, scalable big data store for Hadoop. Inspired by Google's Bigtable technology, HBase serves as the underlying infrastructure for much of the consumer web.
When:
Thursday, June 13, 2013
Where:
Marriott Marquis, 55 Fourth St., San Francisco, CA 94103
Who:
Keynote speakers include:

Michael Stack, software engineer, Cloudera and HBase Project Management Committee Chair
Lars Hofhansl, architect, Salesforce.com and HBase Project Management Committee Member
Liyin Tang, software engineer, Facebook and HBase Project Management Committee Member
Mike Olson, chief executive officer, Cloudera
Aaron Kimball, chief technology officer, WibiData

Sponsors include:

Event: Continuuity, Hortonworks, MapR, NGData, Opower, Salesforce.com and WibiData
Community: Apache Software Foundation
Media: O'Reilly Media and Manning Publications

Registration and Sponsorship Information
For more information about HBaseCon 2013, visit: https://www.hbasecon.com/, or register at: https://hbasecon13-es2.eventbrite.com/.
For more information about becoming a sponsor at HBaseCon, visit: https://www.hbasecon.com/sponsors/. Sponsorship opportunities are open until May 15, 2013.
Discounts on Apache HBase Training and Materials for HBaseCon 2013 Attendees
HBaseCon 2013 attendees are invited to purchase discounted Apache HBase training from Cloudera, which will take place June 11 - 12 in San Francisco. Attendees are also eligible for deep discounts on best-selling HBase books from O'Reilly and Manning Publications. For more information, visit: https://www.hbasecon.com/apache-hbase-training-discount-for-hbasecon-attendees/.

About Cloudera
Cloudera is revolutionizing enterprise data management by offering the first unified Platform for big data, an enterprise data hub built on Apache Hadoop. Cloudera offers enterprises one place to store, access, process, secure, and analyze all their data, empowering them to extend the value of existing investments while enabling fundamental new ways to derive value from their data. Cloudera's open source big data platform is the most widely adopted in the world, and Cloudera is the most prolific contributor to the open source Hadoop ecosystem. As the leading educator of Hadoop professionals, Cloudera has trained over 40,000 individuals worldwide. Over 1,700 partners and a seasoned professional services team help deliver greater time to value. Leading organizations in every industry plus top public sector organizations globally run Cloudera in production.
