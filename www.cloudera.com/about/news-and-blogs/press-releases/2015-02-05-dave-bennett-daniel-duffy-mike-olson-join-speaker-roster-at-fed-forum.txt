Press Release
Dave Bennett, Daniel Duffy and Mike Olson Join Speaker Roster at 4th Annual Cloudera Federal Forum














Event Drives Dialogue about Business and Technical Strategies to Leverage Today's Big Data Platforms and Applications
MEDIA ALERT: MCLEAN, VA. – February 5, 2015 –
What: Today, Cloudera Government Solutions, Inc. announced the speakers and sessions scheduled for the 2015 Cloudera Federal Forum, "Manage, Analyze, and Act: How Big Data is Changing Agency Outcomes," taking place February 25, 2015. The event, geared towards public sector professionals, will enable a dialogue about business and technical strategies for leveraging today's big data platforms and applications to the advantage of public sector agencies.
Who: Defense Information Systems Agency chief information officer Dave Bennett, Cloudera co-founder and chief strategy officer Mike Olson, and NASA Center for Climate Simulation head Daniel Duffy have been confirmed as speakers.
Bennett, in addition to his role as CIO, is the principal director for Enterprise Information Services for the Defense Information Systems Agency. Mr. Bennett has earned numerous military awards, including the Defense Superior Service medal and the Legion of Merit. He will keynote the forum from 8:15 to 9:00 a.m.
Duffy has worked on a number of NASA projects exploring technologies for the next generation of high performance computing solutions, including being the co-investigator on the MERRA Analytics Service Project, which has led to the formulation of Climate Analytics-as-a-Service. Before joining NASA, Duffy worked on highly parallel applications for the Department of Defense.
Olson, who co-founded Cloudera in 2008 and served as its CEO until 2013, today serves as chief strategy officer, where he drives the company's product strategy, open-source leadership, engineering alignment, and direct engagement with customers. Prior to Cloudera, Olson was CEO of Sleepycat Software and vice president for Embedded Technologies of Oracle. He will keynote from 9:00 to 9:30 a.m.
Other confirmed speakers include:

Eddie Garcia, Chief Security Architect, Office of the CTO, Cloudera
Webster Mudge, Senior Director of Technology Solutions, Cloudera
Greg Jones, Director, Deloitte Consulting LLP
Freemon Johnson, Senior Computer Scientist, Department of State
Robert Ames, Senior Vice President, Advanced Analytics Practice, In-Q-Tel
Steve Orrin, Chief Technologist, Intel Federal LLC, Intel Corporation
Mike Sutten, Senior Vice President and Chief Technology Officer, Kaiser Permanente
Nick Curcuru, Senior Business Leader, MasterCard
Bob Jimenez, Chief Technology Officer, National Reconnaissance Office

When: Wednesday, February 25, 2015 from 8:00 a.m. to 3:45 p.m. ET.
Where: Hilton McLean Tysons Corner, 7920 Jones Branch Drive, McLean, Virginia 22102

McLean Metro Station: Silver Line
Free Shuttles Provided

Registration and Sponsorship Information

About Cloudera
Cloudera is revolutionizing enterprise data management by offering the first unified Platform for big data, an enterprise data hub built on Apache Hadoop. Cloudera offers enterprises one place to store, access, process, secure, and analyze all their data, empowering them to extend the value of existing investments while enabling fundamental new ways to derive value from their data. Cloudera's open source big data platform is the most widely adopted in the world, and Cloudera is the most prolific contributor to the open source Hadoop ecosystem. As the leading educator of Hadoop professionals, Cloudera has trained over 40,000 individuals worldwide. Over 1,700 partners and a seasoned professional services team help deliver greater time to value. Leading organizations in every industry plus top public sector organizations globally run Cloudera in production.
