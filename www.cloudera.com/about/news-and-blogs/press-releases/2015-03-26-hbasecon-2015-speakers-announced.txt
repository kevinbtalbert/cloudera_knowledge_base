HBaseCon 2015 Speakers Announced






 









Users of World’s Largest Production HBase Deployments Present at Annual Community Conference Hosted by Cloudera
MEDIA ALERT: PALO ALTO, CA. -- March 26, 2015 --
What: Today, the HBaseCon 2015 Program Committee announced the speaker lineup and agenda for the fourth annual HBaseCon, the premier community event for Apache HBase contributors, developers, admins, and users of all skill levels. The full-day event includes keynotes, breakout sessions, and networking opportunities.
HBaseCon was founded and is hosted/organized by Cloudera, with Program Committee representation from Cask Data, Cloudera, Facebook, Google, Hortonworks, and Salesforce.com.
Who: Keynotes include:

“State of HBase” (Andrew Purtell, Salesforce.com; Enis Söztutar, Hortonworks; Michael Stack, Cloudera)

“Bigtable and HBase: Storing the World's Data” (Carter Page, Google)

“Zen: A Graph Data Model on HBase” (Raghavendra Prabhu and Xun Liu, Pinterest)

“The Evolution of HBase @ Bloomberg” (Matthew Hunt and Sudarshan Kadambi, Bloomberg)


Session speakers are developers and operators of some of the world’s most impressive production HBase deployments, including those in use at:

Adobe

Bloomberg

Cerner

CyberAgent

Dropbox

eBay

Facebook

FINRA

Flipboard

Google

Pinterest

Rocket Fuel

Salesforce.com

Xiaomi

Yahoo!


Sponsors of HBaseCon include:

Gold/Silver: Bloomberg, Cask Data, Google, Hortonworks, MapR, Salesforce.com

Community: Apache Software Foundation, The Hive Think Tank

Media: O’Reilly Media


When: Thursday, May 7, 2015, 7.30am-8.00pm PT
Where: The Westin St. Francis on Union Square, 335 Powell Street, San Francisco, California, 94102
Registration, Agenda, and Sponsorship Information: For more information about HBaseCon 2015, visit: https://hbasecon.com.
HBase Training for HBaseCon 2015 Attendees:
For those interested in Cloudera University training for Apache HBase, the company is offering a three-day training class in San Francisco prior to the conference from May 4-6, 2015. HBaseCon attendees are eligible for a 15% discount on this training session only.

About Cloudera
Cloudera is revolutionizing enterprise data management by offering the first unified Platform for big data, an enterprise data hub built on Apache Hadoop. Cloudera offers enterprises one place to store, access, process, secure, and analyze all their data, empowering them to extend the value of existing investments while enabling fundamental new ways to derive value from their data. Cloudera's open source big data platform is the most widely adopted in the world, and Cloudera is the most prolific contributor to the open source Hadoop ecosystem. As the leading educator of Hadoop professionals, Cloudera has trained over 40,000 individuals worldwide. Over 1,700 partners and a seasoned professional services team help deliver greater time to value. Leading organizations in every industry plus top public sector organizations globally run Cloudera in production.
