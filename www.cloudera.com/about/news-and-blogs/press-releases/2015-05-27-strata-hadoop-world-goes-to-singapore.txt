Strata + Hadoop World Goes to Singapore; Cloudera and O’Reilly Media Present Their First Data Event in Asia, 1-3 December 2015






 









New Big Data Event Addresses Burgeoning Industry Interest and Demand for Data Management in Asia
MEDIA ALERT: PALO ALTO, Calif. – May 27 2015 –
What: Today, Cloudera, Inc. the leader in analytic data management announced the first-ever Singapore edition of the Strata + Hadoop World conference. Presented by Cloudera and O’Reilly Media, Strata + Hadoop World is where big data's most influential business decision makers, strategists, architects, developers, and analysts gather to shape the future of their businesses and technologies. The three-day event includes keynote sessions, tutorials, training, technology showcases, and networking opportunities.
Who: Keynote speakers will include executives from Cloudera, O’Reilly Media, and other key thought leaders including big data users.
When: Tuesday, December 1, 2015 through Thursday, December 3, 2015. For the complete conference schedule, visit www.stratahadoopworld.com/singapore/index.html.
Where: Suntec Singapore Convention and Exhibition Centre, 1 Raffles Boulevard, Suntec City, Singapore 039593.
Apply to Speak: The Call for Presenters is open now and we're looking for speakers to share case studies, best practices, and technical skills-- especially those that can showcase the opportunities and challenges of the Asian market.
One reason for the success of Strata + Hadoop World is the caliber and diversity of our speakers: people with a passion for data and expertise in the most urgent and interesting areas of data. Deadline is June 18th. For more information on submissions, visit www.stratahadoopworld.com/singapore/index.html
Registration Information: Conference registration opens in mid-July. Use discount code CLOUDERA20 to save 20% off registration. For more information about Strata + Hadoop World Singapore 2015, visit www.stratahadoopworld.com/singapore/index.html
Training for Strata + Hadoop World Singapore 2015 Attendees: Conference attendees have the option to attend training at the conference on data exploration, analysis, and building big data applications. Full training schedule available soon.
To learn more about Cloudera’s presence at Strata + Hadoop World Singapore or, visit www.stratahadoopworld.com/singapore/index.html.

About O'Reilly
O'Reilly Media spreads the knowledge of innovators through its books, online services, magazines, and conferences. Since 1978, O'Reilly Media has been a chronicler and catalyst of cutting-edge development, homing in on the technology trends that really matter and spurring their adoption by amplifying "faint signals" from the alpha geeks who are creating the future. An active participant in the technology community, the company has a long history of advocacy, meme-making, and evangelism.






About Cloudera
Cloudera is revolutionizing enterprise data management by offering the first unified Platform for big data, an enterprise data hub built on Apache Hadoop. Cloudera offers enterprises one place to store, access, process, secure, and analyze all their data, empowering them to extend the value of existing investments while enabling fundamental new ways to derive value from their data. Cloudera's open source big data platform is the most widely adopted in the world, and Cloudera is the most prolific contributor to the open source Hadoop ecosystem. As the leading educator of Hadoop professionals, Cloudera has trained over 40,000 individuals worldwide. Over 1,700 partners and a seasoned professional services team help deliver greater time to value. Leading organizations in every industry plus top public sector organizations globally run Cloudera in production.
