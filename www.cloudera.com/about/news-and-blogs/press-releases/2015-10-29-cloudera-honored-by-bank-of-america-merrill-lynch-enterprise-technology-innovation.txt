Cloudera Honored by Bank of America Merrill Lynch for Enterprise Technology Innovation






 









Cloudera Recognized for Its Industry Leadership in Analytic Data Management Technology
PALO ALTO, Calif., October 29, 2015 – Cloudera, the global provider of the fastest, easiest, and most secure data management and analytics platform built on Apache(TM) Hadoop(R) and the latest open source technologies, was honored by Bank of America Merrill Lynch during this year’s seventh annual Technology Innovation Summit in Silicon Valley for its innovation in enterprise technology. Cloudera was chosen as the sole recipient of this year’s honor for its industry leadership in new ways to manage, analyze, and gain value from data.
 
Cloudera helps organizations store, process, and analyze all enterprise data, regardless of type and volume, thereby reducing costs and enabling business transformation. With over 7001 global enterprise and public sector customers, and an ecosystem of more than 1,900 partners, Cloudera is widely recognized as a leading innovator and the industry standard in enterprise Hadoop. It is also the largest contributor to the Hadoop open source software community.
 
“At Cloudera, we’ve been leading the way in helping organizations across a wide range of industries harness the power of next generation analytics and rethink data management,” said Tom Reilly, chief executive officer, Cloudera. “We’re honored to be recognized by Bank of America Merrill Lynch for our leadership in enterprise technology, and will continue to push forward to create high value business solutions that put data at the center of operations.”
 
About Cloudera
Cloudera delivers the modern data management and analytics platform built on Apache Hadoop and the latest open source technologies. The world’s leading organizations trust Cloudera to help solve their most challenging business problems with Cloudera Enterprise, the fastest, easiest and most secure data platform available for the modern world. Our customers efficiently capture, store, process and analyze vast amounts of data, empowering them to use advanced analytics to drive business decisions quickly, flexibly and at lower cost than has been possible before. To ensure our customers are successful, we offer comprehensive support, training and professional services. Learn more at https://cloudera.com.
