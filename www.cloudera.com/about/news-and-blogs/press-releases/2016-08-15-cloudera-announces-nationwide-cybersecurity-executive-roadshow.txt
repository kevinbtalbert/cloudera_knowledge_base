Cloudera Announces Nationwide Cybersecurity Executive Roadshow



 









Cloudera Chief Strategy Officer and Co-Founder Mike Olson will kick off the New York leg of its eight-city cybersecurity executive roadshow.
NEW YORK, N.Y., August 15, 2016 — Cloudera, the global provider of the fastest, easiest, and most secure data management and analytics platform built on Apache Hadoop and the latest open source technologies, today announced that Cloudera Chief Strategy Officer and Co-Founder Mike Olson will kick off the New York leg of its eight-city cybersecurity executive roadshow with a keynote focused on how Apache Hadoop is being used to strengthen enterprise cybersecurity. Joining Olson at the event will be Intel Senior Principal Engineer Alan Ross, Accenture Senior Principal Patrick Joyce, and Ovum Principal Analyst Andrew Kellett. Executive attendees at the event will:

Discover how Apache Hadoop is being used to secure one of the largest technology companies in the world
Learn about how artificial intelligence, big data analytics, and Cloudera-based intelligence platforms are helping G2000 companies to reduce risk and detect incidents
Understand what some of the most pressing cybersecurity challenges are today and have an opportunity to discuss them in an open round-table forum


The New York event will be held on Wednesday, Aug. 17 at Cipriani’s Library room, 55 Wall Street. The event runs from 8:30 a.m. to 11:30 a.m. ET. 
In addition to New York, Cloudera will be hosting additional events featuring speakers including Olson, Cloudera CEO Tom Reilly, and Rocky DeStefano, Cloudera cybersecurity subject matter expert and former member of the USAF AFCERT Incident Response Team. Additional locations include: 

Boston, MA, Aug. 23, 2016
Minneapolis, MN, Oct. 6, 2016
Dallas, TX, Oct. 19, 2016
Atlanta, GA, Oct. 25, 2016
Washington DC, Oct. 26, 2016
Seattle, WA, Nov. 2, 2016
San Francisco, CA, Nov. 3, 2016

Journalists Invited
Media representatives are also welcome to attend. Interested writers and broadcasters should contact Deborah Wiltshire at press@cloudera.com or at +1.650.644.3900. 
 






About Cloudera
Cloudera is revolutionizing enterprise data management by offering the first unified Platform for big data, an enterprise data hub built on Apache Hadoop. Cloudera offers enterprises one place to store, access, process, secure, and analyze all their data, empowering them to extend the value of existing investments while enabling fundamental new ways to derive value from their data. Cloudera's open source big data platform is the most widely adopted in the world, and Cloudera is the most prolific contributor to the open source Hadoop ecosystem. As the leading educator of Hadoop professionals, Cloudera has trained over 40,000 individuals worldwide. Over 1,700 partners and a seasoned professional services team help deliver greater time to value. Leading organizations in every industry plus top public sector organizations globally run Cloudera in production.
