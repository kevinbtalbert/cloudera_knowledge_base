Cloudera and Telkomsigma Partner to Drive Business Value in Indonesia with Big Data Analytics



 









Strategic partnership aims to empower Indonesian organizations with deeper customer insights and new competitive advantages
JAKARTA, Indonesia, November 8, 2016 — Cloudera, the global provider of the fastest, easiest, and most secure data management and analytics platform built on Apache Hadoop and the latest open source technologies, today announced a strategic partnership with Telkomsigma, the leading provider of integrated ICT solutions in Indonesia, to deliver big data analytics services and solutions to Telkomsigma’s customers. As a key partner of Cloudera in Indonesia and Asia Pacific, Telkomsigma will offer a fully managed big data analytics solution powered by Cloudera Enterprise.
As Indonesia continues its journey towards rapid digitization and enhanced connectivity, businesses are gearing up to strengthen their value chain through the adoption of big data analytics technology. Increasingly, organizations in the country are tapping into big data to derive deeper insights and create new competitive advantages.
Cloudera and Telkomsigma will lay the ground work for a robust big data analytics infrastructure for both the public and private sector organizations along with full support services, as well as, skills training to accelerate business growth in this partnership.
“Cloudera has been working with Indonesian businesses and providing unparalleled support in analytics infrastructure, engineering, services, consulting, and training, for over two years now,” said Fred Groen, Country Manager, Cloudera, Indonesia.
“Moving forward, Cloudera and Telkomsigma will jointly help enterprises here develop a 360-degree customer view, data-warehousing, database modernization, cybersecurity analytics, and Internet of Things (IoT) data consumption solutions. This partnership signals our continued commitment to the Indonesian market,” added Groen.
Telkomsigma aims to work with businesses and organizations in Indonesia and help them to strive for the next level of growth by implementing data analytics use cases that drive business value.
“Indonesia is one of the fastest growing consumer markets for the mobile and banking sectors, amongst several other industries, and service providers are already at the forefront of the nation’s digital transformation with their rapid adoption of big data analytics platforms such as Cloudera’s,” said Andreuw Th.A.F, Director of  Business Data Center and Managed Services, Telkomsigma.
“Through this partnership with Cloudera, we are committed to taking big data to the next level for Indonesian businesses. We are confident that we will be providing our customers with the fastest, easiest, and most secure platform that they can use to derive the most value from their data,” Th.A.F further said.
Telkomsigma has already put together a team of data scientists, engineers, and solution architects to support the deployment of Cloudera solutions. “Telkomsigma and Cloudera share a common goal of empowering enterprises in Indonesia and the Asia Pacific region with data analytics capabilities, and working with them to derive significant business value and positive transformation,” said Fanly Tanto, Director of Sales, Indonesia, Cloudera.
“With our solution, joint initiatives, and Telkomsigma’s strong presence here, we aim to strive towards a greater awareness and appreciation of solutions like advanced analytics, machine learning, and real-time analytics, together acting as the catalysts for accelerated market growth,” Tanto emphasized.
Cloudera counts recognizable Indonesian companies like Bank Danamon and Telkomsel as its key customers. Telkomsel is also one of the largest Hadoop users and power Cloudera user across Southeast Asia. Cloudera has also recently signed Telkom University as a key education partner to provide education through the university system in Indonesia.
