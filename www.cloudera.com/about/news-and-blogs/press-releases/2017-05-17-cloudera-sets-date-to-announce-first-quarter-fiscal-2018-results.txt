Press Release
Cloudera Sets Date to Announce First Quarter Fiscal 2018 Results



 









 
PALO ALTO, Calif. – May 17, 2017: Cloudera, Inc. (NYSE:CLDR), provider of the leading modern platform for machine learning and advanced analytics, today announced that it will report its first quarter fiscal 2018 (ended April 30, 2017) financial results on June 8, 2017, after the close of market, and host a conference call to discuss the results at 2.00pm Pacific Time (5.00pm Eastern Time).
Conference Call & Webcast Information
The conference call can be accessed as follows:

Participant Toll Free Dial-In Number: 1-877-201-0168
Participant International Dial-In Number: +1-647-788-4901
Conference ID: 25758133

A webcast of the conference call can be accessed from the News & Events section of the Company’s website at https://investors.cloudera.com/news-and-events/events/default.aspx. The webcast will continue to be available for two weeks following completion of the call.






About Cloudera
Cloudera delivers the modern platform for machine learning and advanced analytics built on the latest open source technologies. The world’s leading organizations trust Cloudera to help solve their most challenging business problems. Learn more at cloudera.com.
