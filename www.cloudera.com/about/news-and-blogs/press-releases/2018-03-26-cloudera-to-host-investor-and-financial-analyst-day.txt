Cloudera to Host Investor and Financial Analyst Day









PALO ALTO, Calif. – March 26, 2018 – Cloudera, Inc. (NYSE: CLDR), the modern platform for machine learning and analytics optimized for the cloud, announced that it will host an Investor and Financial Analyst Day on April 12, 2018 in New York City.
Cloudera’s executive and senior management team will provide an overview of the company’s strategy and business, with presentations beginning at 9:00 am ET and concluding at approximately 3:00 pm ET. A live webcast and replay of the event will be accessible from the company’s website at https://investors.cloudera.com.
Attendance is by invitation only as space for the event is limited. Institutional investors and financial analysts interested in attending are encouraged to contact Investor Relations at investor-relations@cloudera.com.
About Cloudera
At Cloudera, we believe that data can make what is impossible today, possible tomorrow. We empower people to transform complex data into clear and actionable insights. We deliver the modern platform for machine learning and analytics optimized for the cloud. The world's largest enterprises trust Cloudera to help solve their most challenging business problems. Learn more at cloudera.com.
