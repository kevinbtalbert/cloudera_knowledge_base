Training Credits Redemption Guide














Cloudera Education offers a complete portfolio of courses and certifications that can be redeemed using your training credits. Below you will find helpful guidelines for redeeming those credits and the steps required to request and/or enroll in a class or an exam.
Training Credit Policy: All redemptions for training or certification products must be scheduled and delivered no later than the training credit expiration date.

Training/certification product expirations may be non-standard when using training credits as the payment method.

Cloudera training/certification products purchased via purchase order will be valid for 1 year from the purchase date.


 
Instructor-Led Training Classes:
We offer virtual and live classroom training globally.  Public and private training is available.   Our public class schedule is available at https://www.cloudera.com/about/training/course-directory.html.  Private classes can be delivered virtually or on site at a customer's facility.
To enroll in a public class using training credits, contact EDU_OPS_INTERNAL@cloudera.com and provide the following:

course name, location and start date

student name

student email (company)

student country of residence


To schedule a private event, contact EDU_OPS_INTERNAL@cloudera.com .
 
OnDemand Training (video-based e-Learning):
Our OnDemand platform gives students access to our self-paced video-based curriculum and hands-on exercises in a remotely-hosted environment. Visit https://www.cloudera.com/about/training/courses/cloudera-ondemand-library.html for detailed information and pricing. Note that not all courses include hands-on lab environments.

To purchase an OnDemand subscription using training credits, contact ondemand@cloudera.com and provide the following:

student name

student email (company)

student country of residence




 
Certification Exams:
Cloudera provides certifications for individuals seeking to advance their careers and prove their mastery with Cloudera technology. For a listing of the certifications offered visit https://www.cloudera.com/about/training/certification.html .

To make a certification exam purchase using training credits, contact certification@cloudera.com and provide the following:

exam name

candidate name

candidate email (company)

candidate country of residence
