      Overview
      




Cloudera is the modern platform for machine learning and analytics, optimized for the cloud. We are your partner on your organization's data-driven journey. However, without training, it is difficult to fully derive value from all your data. The most successful big data organizations are staffed by trained experts who can leverage best practices to execute faster and more effectively.
Learn from the insights Cloudera has gained over years of supporting the world’s data-driven customers. Cloudera University provides learning paths for developers, administrators, data analysts. and data scientists to go beyond the fundamentals and cross-train to achieve true expertise. Follow your professional learning path to master the skills your role requires.









 




















 







    
    
      For Developers
      




Build game-changing big data applications and future-proof your career. Learn everything you need to become a leader in the field, from architecture and ecosystem fundamentals to Spark and Hadoop programming, real-time analytics, and data science.








         Become a Cloudera Developer
         




















    
    
      For Administrators
      




Scale the value of big data from system strategy to high-value analytics. Master the elements of cluster monitoring, governance, security, and troubleshooting, and become a certified professional.








         Become a Cloudera Administrator
         










 




















 







    
    
      For Data Analysts
      




Analyze big data at the speed of thought using real-time SQL query and data science. Perform critical analyses, drive business intelligence, and develop sophisticated tools on massive data sets utilizing the full Cloudera ecosystem.








         Become a Cloudera Data Analyst
