Cloudera training partners






 









    
    
      Bringing big data education to the world
      




Increasing demand for professionals with the skills to derive value from big data requires expert training to equip big data professionals with the tools and training to manage, process, analyze, and monetize more data than they ever thought possible.









 





















 







    
    
      Partnership designed for success
      




Cloudera training partnerships are designed to promote big data technical education, training, and certification around the world. Cloudera partners with industry-leading training delivery organizations, training resellers, and academic institutions to increase the accessibility of this instruction and expand the global reach of Cloudera's curricula.
Cloudera’s courseware delivers a thorough overview of the big data ecosystem, role-specific technical instruction aligned with professional certification exams, and use-case scenarios, all complemented by the industry's most relevant hands-on exercises, labs, and virtual machine learning environment.





















    
    
      Who should join
      




The Cloudera training partnerships are created with various training channels in mind. Our best-of-breed program offers three unique tracks to ensure support and benefits aligned to your objectives and also provide confidence to both your customers and students that they are receiving training authorized and approved by Cloudera:
