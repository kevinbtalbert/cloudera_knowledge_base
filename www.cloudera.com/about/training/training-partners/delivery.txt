Add Cloudera big data training to your education portfolio
Cloudera is transforming the way organizations in every vertical interact with their data, their markets, and their customers. Gartner predicts sustained growth in the big data market. These trends point towards continued adoption and the persistent need for trained, certified professionals to deliver on tremendous value of an organization's data.
Join Cloudera Connect
Become an Authorized Cloudera Training Partner to provide your customers with the expertise they need to become leaders in the new data economy. Partners with the technical and instructional proficiency to deliver Cloudera training in all geographies will be critical to the growth and success of the big data market. Our goal is to work with the most respected training organizations in the industry and to create substantial revenue opportunities.
Contact Us


























Benefits of the Partnership
Cloudera offers an unrivaled breadth and depth of products and services to enterprises looking to harness the power of all their data. Our partners have a unique opportunity to take advantage of Cloudera's leadership and expertise in the big data space. As an Authorized Cloudera Training Partner, you have access to a broad set of technical and business resources to help you build and differentiate your training practice:

Training Materials — Conduct classes using the most comprehensive and state-of-the-art course content
Sales Channels — Be showcased among leading big data training providers, offering customers registration and enrollment access through the Cloudera University website
Partner Portal — Access a complete set of technical, sales, and marketing tools and resources
Technical and Sales Training — Get specialized instruction on Cloudera's training materials and methods, including regular updates and upgrades plus free attendance for prospective instructors at any Cloudera-delivered training course
Marketing Support — Leverage high-quality assets to drive demand and collaborate with Cloudera marketers dedicated to proliferating training sales opportunities
Technical Support — Collaborate with the entire global network of Cloudera instructors and receive direct programmatic support from Cloudera University management
