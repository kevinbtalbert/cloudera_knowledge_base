Data Warehouse Modernization



















Beyond the traditional data warehouse
As the demands of today’s data warehouse users evolve and the need for more analytics and new types of data increases, we see the current infrastructure being pushed beyond its original design.
If any of the following apply to you, now is the time to consider modernizing your data warehouse infrastructure to efficiently meet the needs of today’s data citizens.

Are you looking for data warehousing in the public cloud?
Do you have any workloads that are not performing as expected?
Have you reached limits in storage, user connections or other capacities on your current infrastructure?















Are you struggling with including new data streams like log and IoT that you need for meeting business operational improvement analytics?
Are you looking for a way to analyze unstructured and structured data together?
Are you doing exploratory analytics and need more data for better future predictions?
Are you expanding into AI/ML with your data warehousing projects?
