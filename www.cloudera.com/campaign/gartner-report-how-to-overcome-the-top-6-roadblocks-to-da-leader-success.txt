Understand the constraints to a data leader’s success and a roadmap to overcome them













According to Gartner, “Data and analytics leaders need to deliver tangible business outcome for their organizations, but they struggle to show value. To deliver value from their data and analytics initiatives, leaders must iteratively develop data and analytics capabilities.” But based on the finding of nearly 500 chief data and analytics officers (CDAOs) in the Gartner Chief Data Officer Agenda Survey for 2022, various roadblocks act as inhibitors to their success.










 



 







 



Download this Gartner report to:

Learn about the top roadblocks impeding success for CDOs and their organizations. 

Get practical advice on how to build a D&A roadmap to avoid these roadblocks. 

Understand how to overcome culture change challenges. 

Discover how data and analytics contributes to the data-driven organization and drives business outcomes.
