ANALYST REPORT






The opportunities and data management considerations of hybrid cloud













 


















Learn strategies to ensure hybrid cloud success






Using multiple cloud services from multiple cloud providers is not the same thing as having a hybrid cloud strategy. Many enterprises have stumbled into the use of multi-cloud environments thanks to tactical, departmentally driven decentralized approaches to cloud service adoption as well as shadow IT efforts.













As more companies see the benefits of operating multiple infrastructure environments (both on- and off-premises), an increasing number of enterprises are adopting strategies that enable and encourage hybrid cloud adoption.










 



 







 



This 451 Research Business Impact Brief discusses how to best approach hybrid cloud adoption, explaining:

The business benefits of multiple cloud environments
The challenges of distributed data management
The key services required to manage data across multiple environments
