Streams Messaging Solution Kit






Cloudera DataFlow’s Streams Messaging capabilities address data management challenges with streaming and other IoT data at scale.  It empowers enterprises to ingest and buffer millions of streaming data inputs and quickly build real-time use cases.
This solution kit includes resources to help you learn more about:














How CDP’s streams messaging enables faster real-time insight across the firm.
How Cloudera’s Kafka ecosystem ensures an end-to-end operational awareness across producers, brokers and consumers.
 How enterprises can unify security and governance to ensure policy and compliance are enforced across all users
