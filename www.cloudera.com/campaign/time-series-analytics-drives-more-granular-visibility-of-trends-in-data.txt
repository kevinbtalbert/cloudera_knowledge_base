Time series analytics drives more granular visibility of trends in data













 


















Learn about Cloudera's perspective on time series analytics






The explosive growth in time series data is being driven by advances in sensors, connections, databases, online systems, and analytics tools. Time series analytics provides business insights that enable firms to forecast and predict the future. Outlook Series' Michael Lippis interviews Eva Nahari to gain Cloudera's perspective on time series analytics.They address many key topics including:

The definition of time series data and who needs time series analytics
How processing power impacts time series data
How important is it to be able to slice and dice time series data
How can the Cloudera Data Science Workbench be utilized with time series data
