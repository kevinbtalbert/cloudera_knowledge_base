Explore opportunities at Cloudera
We’re on a mission to make data and analytics easy and accessible to everyone. The work we do empowers any organization – from the world's largest enterprises to small nonprofits –  to use data to solve some of the most complex challenges that impact businesses, communities and lives.











 















The Power of We
We’re a global team of diverse talent driving change and impact among some of the top organizations across every industry. From the top 10 in Telecommunications to the top 8 in FinTech and the top 9 in Healthcare, we’re using data and disruptive technology to make what is impossible today, possible tomorrow.
Engineering, Marketing, Human Resources, Sales and Support teammates work together in 31 countries across the world ensuring that our open source technologies are driving change and innovation.
What can you make possible when you join the Power of We?

#ClouderaLife
At Cloudera, we’re Customer Driven & People First! That means that while we’re working hard to solve our clients' most complex data challenges, we’re also making sure that every Clouderan feels valued, has growth opportunities, and that their well being is a priority.

Corporate Social Responsibility
Giving back has always been an integral part of the Cloudera culture. Through Cloudera Cares, our giving and volunteering program, our employees are encouraged and supported to volunteer and give to organizations where they live and work. It offers Clouderans with volunteering incentives, support and toolkits to aid more organizations in local regions.








 Learn more














 



 




Diversity, Equality & Inclusion
At Cloudera, we’re encouraged to bring our full selves to work each day.  With a DEI team that conducts regular “Listening Circles” and various initiatives to improve diversity and allyship, and 11 different Employee Resource Groups (ERG) such as CBEN (Cloudera’s Black Employee Network), WomenX, LGBTQ+ and more,  you’ll find that you belong here.
