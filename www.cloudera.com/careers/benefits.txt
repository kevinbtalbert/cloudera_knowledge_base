We support Clouderans in their career development through a variety of methods.
We provide Clouderans diverse career development opportunities, including custom training, workshops, and toolkits for all levels.Topics include feedback, communication, and leadership.
We offer access to Udemy's 5000+ courses, from tech certs to personal growth, along with an Education Reimbursement program. Our goal: empower all through a culture of learning.










 



 







 



























Cloudera Unplugged Days
At Cloudera, we take the mental & physical health of our employees seriously & always encourage you to take time off to rest and recharge where needed. To support this belief, Cloudera will continue our series of company Unplug Days. These Unplug Days are granted as paid time off in the form of a Company holiday and as a benefit to our employees and will be classified as standalone Company holidays.
