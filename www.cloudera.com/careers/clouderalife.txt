What we do
We’re a global team of top notch talent driving change and impact among some of the top organizations across every industry. We’re using data and disruptive technology to make what is impossible today, possible tomorrow. 
From helping non profits reduce childhood malnutrition to aiding police forces in proactively serving their communities, we empower leaders in their industries to transform data into actionable insights through our hybrid data platform.

Cloudera Values
At Cloudera, our culture, our ethos, is centered on our values. We're here not only because we're experts at what we do, but also because we hold these values in common
Equality: Collaborating to ensure diverse perspectives are represented in each conversation to understand how representation at all levels matters.
Commitment: Prioritizing and delivering high quality results quickly: to our customers, our communities, our teams, and ourselves.
Integrity: Approaching interactions with good intentions, honesty, transparency, and respect.
Taking risks: Pushing past ‘what’s always been done’ in order to find what was never thought possible.
