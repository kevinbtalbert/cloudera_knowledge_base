The real role of robotic automation in retail - a benchmark report





                    Retail and CPG digital transformation is moving at breakneck speeds offering improvements to the buying experience and retail’s supply chain.  Retail is employing robotics and automation in-store and in distribution centers to drive efficiencies, worker productivity and improve in-store merchandising.   Cloudera and its' partner, RSR (Retail Systems Research) is providing insights that will help you understand where this technology is going, who is driving the transformation, and what are the expected results through  a survey of sixty-six qualified retail respondents providing a comprehensive view of robotic automation deployment.  Cloudera can help you develop a data driven approach through the lens of the data lifecycle to deploy this technology
