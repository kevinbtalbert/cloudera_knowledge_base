Dell SecureWorks slashes the cost of storage with Dell | Cloudera Hadoop solution





                    In the time it takes to read this brief article, Dell SecureWorks will have collected and analyzed information on millions of IT events that took place during the same interval at thousands of customer sites around the world. This is all part of the work of an information security services organization that never sleeps. Dell SecureWorks is on deck 24 hours a day, 365 days a year, to help protect the security of its customers’ assets in real time.  To meet its enormous data processing challenges, Dell SecureWorks requires leading-edge technologies for cost-effectively storing, scaling, and analyzing massive amounts of information in real time. And that’s just what it found in the Dell | Cloudera® Solution for high-speed data analysis, mining, and processing.
