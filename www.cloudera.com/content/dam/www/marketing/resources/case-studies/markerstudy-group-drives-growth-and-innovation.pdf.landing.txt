Markerstudy Group Drives Growth and Innovation





                    The growth of cloud-based solutions, digital, and Internet of Things (IoT) requires a new approach to accessing and analyzing data of all types, age, and volume. Learn how Markerstudy Group, a leading UK-based general insurer, is driving growth and innovation with its big data analytics platform. The insurer uses both Cloudera Enterprise powered by Hadoop and SAS Analytics. With its big data analytics platform, Markerstudy has achieved such results as 120% increase in policy count over 18 months, the ability to tap into new data sources, reduction in claim costs by £5 million through real-time fraud detection and granular customer insights providing a 360-degree view of its policyholders and target markets.
