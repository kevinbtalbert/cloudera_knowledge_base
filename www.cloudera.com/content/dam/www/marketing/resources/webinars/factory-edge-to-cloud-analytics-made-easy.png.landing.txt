Factory Edge to Cloud Analytics Made Easy





                    Industry 4.0 doesn’t need to be a science project – the maturity of the ecosystem allows for each deployment to no longer be a novel one-off adventure, but rather a proven endeavor delivering operational value for established use cases. Cloudera and Litmus have teamed up to deliver this success across the manufacturing industry. Learn more about the emerging intersection between Internet of Things (IoT) data ingest, the data lifecycle and the value it brings to manufacturers. Experienced partnerships such as Cloudera and Litmus offer customers complete Connected Manufacturing solutions, which can be implemented for immediate value without disrupting current processes. Michael Ger, Cloudera Managing Director - Manufacturing, Dinesh Chandrasekhar, Cloudera Head of Data-in-Motion Product Marketing, and John Younes, Litmus Co-Founder and COO, speak on this topic in a panel-style webinar.You will get insight to: Real-world IoT deployments from day 1 through day 60Data lifecycles that will allow full use of IoT and enterprise data, no matter the data source or formatUse cases that have been implemented and can drive value
