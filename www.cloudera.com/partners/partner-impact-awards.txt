2018 Cloudera Partner Impact Awards
Partners are key to our success. Together, we bring data analytics solutions to the world. Congratulations to our Partner Impact Award winners!



























 



Best Solutions Awards - Winners
Did your data solution with Cloudera save lives? Did it turn cyber risks into ROI? Can it predict the future by looking at what’s happening today? If it does, we want to hear all about it and celebrate it. This award recognizes partners who have developed and implemented a point solution that helps our customers gain insights into their businesses. These awards are given in these five sub-categories:



















GROW - Drive Customer Insights
Informatica received the Drive Customer Insights award for driving a solution that breaks down data silos to build a 360-degree view of customers for better marketing, improved loyalty and retention, and/or real-time, personalized recommendations.
CONNECT - Connect Product and Services Efficiency
Streamsets won the Improve Products and Services Efficiency award for driving a solution that drives quantifiable results to organizations by applying data to improve operational efficiencies that generate top-line growth, bottom-line savings, increased competitive advantage, and net new revenue streams.
PROTECT - Lower Business Risks
Booz Allen Hamilton won the Lower Business Risks award for driving a solution that leverages Cloudera to prevent fraud, manage risks, enforce data security, and meet regulatory compliance with tangible results.









TECHNICAL IMPACT - Innovators in Machine Learning
MetiStream received the Innovators in Machine Learning and AI award for driving a solution that is powered by machine learning and artificial intelligence to help to save lives, secure our personal and business information, and engage our customers with better relevance.
TECHNICAL IMPACT - Modernize IT
Accenture won the Modernize IT award for driving a solution that comprises of new and agile technologies built with Cloudera to enable analytics of both old and new data.
 









 




















 



Business Excellence Awards - Winners
The Business Excellence Award recognizes partners at both global and regional levels who have had substantial growth in their customer base and/or revenue by driving customer impact and satisfaction with Cloudera. Partners are selected by Cloudera using internal metrics.



















Partner of the Year Award
Microsoft won the Global Partner of the Year award which recognizes the partner who drove the most Cloudera wins in the past year.
Market Development Award
TCS won the Market Development award, which recognizes the partner who worked with Cloudera to have the strongest year-over-year order revenue growth.
Invest to Win Award
Cognizant won the Invest to Win award which recognizes the partner who is deeply invested in Cloudera and has the largest number of Cloudera certified individuals in the past year to deliver our joint offerings.
Customer Centric Award
Qlik won the Customer-Centric award in recognition of the partner who has the most successful set of publicly reference-able customers.









Innovator Award
Arcadia Data won the Innovator award which recognizes the partner who executed the most effective and creative marketing and sales campaigns with Cloudera.
Rising Star Award
Everis won the Rising Star award which recognizes the partner who joined within the past year that has the best potential in strengthening our joint customer base.  
LATAM Partner of the Year Award
Semantix won the LATAM Partner of the Year award which recognizes the top LATAM Cloudera partner who drove the most business with Cloudera.
North America Partner of the Year Award
Amazon Web Services won the North America Partner of the Year Award which recognizes the top North America Cloudera partner who drove the most business with Cloudera
