Cloudera Solution Competency Program
Connect with partners demonstrating success in enterprise data use cases

 Solution Gallery








 











    
    
      Level up your data strategy with the right business partners
      




Cloudera Connect Solution Competencies highlight Cloudera partners with expertise, technical capabilities, industry knowhow, repeatable solution assets, methodologies, and proven customer success on the Cloudera platform to address specific enterprise data use cases. In addition to competencies that address business problems across many types of Cloudera customers, Industry Competencies highlight industry knowhow and proven customer success on the Cloudera platform in a specific vertical market. 
Partners are all eligible to attain a Solution Competency. Partners with a competency may display a special Solution Competency / Industry Competency badge.









 


































    
    
      Find specific types of data and industry expertise
      




Solution Competency can be attained for categories of business problems that Cloudera customers are seeking solutions. These range from customer analytics to IoT to compliance to technology modernization. Industry Competency are available in the key vertical markets in which Cloudera has a presence. In addition to an evaluation of technical capabilities, Cloudera looks for proven customer success with our products.









 




























    
    
      Take the next step with Cloudera
      




Cloudera makes it easy to apply for a Solution Competency and offers help at every step. And, we invest in promoting and presenting these joint solutions. Benefits for partners with Solution and Industry Competency include:

Joint go-to-market activities
A listing in our Solution Gallery
Exposure to the Cloudera sales team
