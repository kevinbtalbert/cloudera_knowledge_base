      Why Cloudera + Cisco
      




Cloudera and Cisco enable you to gain value from data throughout your business. Cloudera Enterprise runs seamlessly on—and works intelligently with—Cisco UCS Integrated Infrastructure for Big Data. Together, we make it possible to store, protect, and access your data in an industry-compliant environment—wherever and whenever.









 


















      
       
      Joint Solution Overview
      




The strategic use of data is changing. It’s now something we use to make business decisions in real-time. Organizations that successfully leverage data will transform their business and lead their industries.
A Cisco Integrated Infrastructure for big data, powered by Intel® Xeon® processors with Cloudera Enterprise, helps organizations realize the value of their structured, unstructured, and semi-structured data. 

Fast Performance—Access your data quickly to deliver business value through higher performance, faster time-to-insight, and improved scalability

Simple Management—Manage your data and infrastructure easily and efficiently through rapid deployment with minimal effort, simplified single-pane monitoring, and proactive and predictive support 

Robust Security—Ensure your data is well defended with strict authentication and authorization protocols, industry-standard compliance, and complete data encryption

Partnership Highlights

Cisco UCS Integrated Infrastructure for Big Data with Cloudera Enterprise is a fully integrated and validated reference architecture. It is designed to help you gain value from data throughout your business with fast performance, simple management, and robust security
Cisco's Data Warehouse Optimization solution processes data in an intelligent and efficient manner for advanced analytics and insights. The solution also reduces data warehouse management costs by offloading ETL workloads and/or infrequently used data to low-cost Cloudera Enterprise big data clusters.
