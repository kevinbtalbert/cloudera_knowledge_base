      Joint Solution Overview
      




BIGFrame Mainframe Migration
The combination of Cloudera Enterprise with the solutions and services offered by Cognizant’s Enterprise Information Management (EIM) practice creates a strong foundation for successful delivery and accelerated customer time to value.
Cognizant BIGFrame helps organizations migrate their mainframe data and batch applications from a high-cost mainframe environment to a cost-effective and high-performance Cloudera enterprise data hub. The solution includes a web-based tool that accelerates the offloading of mainframe data using Syncsort’s DMX-H. Companies can achieve up to 70 percent savings on mainframe costs using the BIGFrame solution with reduced implementation risk.

BIGDecisions
The BigDecisions Business Solution Platform provides a best-of-breed approach to resolve big data challenges and drive significant benefits. The approach combines business use cases and big data technology solutions for complex data management and analytics that lead to measurable outcomes.
With BigDecisions, clients can expect to:

On-board any new information source for use in 5 - 8 weeks
Provide infrastructure flexibility through various deployment options
