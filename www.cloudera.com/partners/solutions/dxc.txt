      Joint Solution Overview
      




DXC’s Analytics and Big Data Platform
DXC and Cloudera partner to offer businesses a platform for their Big Data needs. Together, they have created the first Hadoop-based platform as a service to receive both PCI and HIPAA certification.  This offers peace of mind and security not just to those with payments or healthcare data, but to any business with sensitive data that they’d like to perform analysis on.  With DXC's Analytics and Big Data Platform, you can minimize risk, increase odds of success, and decrease time-to-value of your big data project.
