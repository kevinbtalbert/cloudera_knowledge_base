Hewlett Packard Enterprise





















 











      
       
      Why Cloudera + HPE?
      




HPE enables enterprises to derive new business insights from big data by providing an infrastructure to store, manage, and process increasing amounts of data . HPE and Cloudera provide performance optimized configurations for deploying Cloudera Enterprise clusters of varying sizes on HPE infrastructure that deliver a significant reduction in complexity with increased value and performance.









 
















HPE Converged Infrastructure provides a strong portfolio of comprehensive big data management tools that when combined with Cloudera’s solutions, bring the benefits of Hadoop to clients faster without the administrative burden.
Paul Miller, Vice President, Converged Application Systems, Enterprise Group, HPE












      
       
      Joint Solution Overview
      




HPE and Cloudera have jointly designed hardware and software configurations to provide optimum computational performance for Hadoop. Cloudera with HPE big data solutions provide best-in-class performance and availability, with integrated software, services, infrastructure and management.
HPE Server Hardware Solutions

HPE Reference Architecture for Cloudera Enterprise on ProLiant DL Servers — HPE and Apache Hadoop allow you to derive new business insights from big data by providing a platform to store, manage, and process data at scale.

HPE Software Solutions

HPE Vertica and Cloudera — Together, HPE Vertica Analytics Platform 7 and Cloudera Enterprise enable deeper real-time analytics into a wider variety of data within a Hadoop environment.  HPE Vertica and Cloudera jointly deliver an end-to-end enterprise data management solution that provides organizations the ability to:

Acquire and combine any amount or type of data in its original fidelity—and, for as long as necessary.
Explore large data sets of structured and unstructured data through multiple forms of data access mechanisms.
Perform real-time data analytics as business objectives and speed requirements dictate.
Support automatic data archive for the complete set of enterprise data, with the convenience of immediate online access, in order to meet compliance requirements.



HPE Services

HPE Haven OnHadoop simplifies and accelerates data exploration—all while running natively on Cloudera Enterprise and leveraging certifications with leading visualization tools.
