      Why Cloudera + Informatica?
      




Informatica’s unique approach to high-performance data integration, high-productivity development, and data-centric security and governance enables organizations to easily find, prepare, govern, and protect data of any size and velocity. By accelerating productivity, enabling IT agility, and driving analyst autonomy, Informatica helps ensure more pervasive and trusted analytics.









 





















As organizations process more sensitive data sets amid ever-changing regulations, significant challenges emerge around data integration. Informatica’s comprehensive suite of big data management solutions provides an integrated solution for turning data into business value. Informatica’s unique integration with Cloudera Navigator allows organizations to get visibility into data lineage inside Hadoop, allowing customers to meet the most challenging compliance requirements.
Ash Parikh, Vice President of Product Marketing, Informatica


















    
    
      Joint Solution Overview
      




Together, Informatica and Cloudera deliver a proven set of solutions for rapidly curating data into trusted information. Informatica’s comprehensive suite of big data management solutions is engineered to run natively on Cloudera Enterprise—taking full advantage of Hadoop’s scalable computing platform. In addition, Informatica’s metadata management capabilities are uniquely integrated with Cloudera Navigator to help provide in-depth visibility into lineage—inside and outside Hadoop—for the most comprehensive compliance with internal and external requirements. Built-in big data governance and big data security make Informatica’s comprehensive suite of management solutions on Cloudera Enterprise a fast, flexible, and enterprise-ready solution for customers looking to turn big data into trusted insights.

      About Informatica
      




Informatica Corporation (NASDAQ:INFA) is the world’s number one independent provider of data integration software.
