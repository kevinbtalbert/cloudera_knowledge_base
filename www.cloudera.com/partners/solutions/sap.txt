      Why Cloudera + SAP?
      




Together, SAP and Cloudera offer enterprises compelling solutions to the challenges of enterprise data management and analytics. With SAP and Cloudera, forward-thinking enterprises can connect next-generation analytical systems like SAP HANA and business intelligence applications like the SAP Lumira and SAP Predictive directly to their Cloudera-powered big data sources.









 


















      
       
      Joint Solution
      




Today, enterprises are at a crossroads in the evolution of their enterprise data architectures. Organizations face the challenge of producing meaningful business value from their growing archives of unstructured data as well as from the expanding array of data sources, both inside and outside the organization.
Together, Cloudera and SAP can help you process, blend, and drive diverse datasets faster to business users, so they can make smarter decisions at the right time and cost.

Partnership Highlights

Combine the speed of SAP HANA in-memory computing with the massive storage and computing scalability of Cloudera Enterprise
