Why Cloudera + Talend?






Talend’s robust data management capabilities and Cloudera’s elastic cloud experience enable organizations to scale and grow their business through data-based decision making. Talend Data Fabric technology and their data health assessment has the first ever process mixing tools and human expertise to improve data quality, enabling customers to unify and trust their data. Talend tightly integrates with Cloudera and has been certified on the latest Cloudera Enterprise Data Cloud. 









 
















Talend and Cloudera have a long history of big data and open source innovation. Together we are helping customers from a range of industries gain a unified view of their business and customers across organizational and technology silos. Talend is optimized for CDP Private Cloud Base and CDP Public Cloud (Data Hub and Data Engineering). Talend’s Data Fabric enables companies to use the latest technologies for data integration, management, and governance to turn data into a strategic asset and competitive strength.
Andy Smith, Vice President, Strategic Alliance & Partner Ecosystems













Joint Solution Overview






Talend Data Fabric environment running with Cloudera CDP enables your data management with a fully integrated, self-service, end-to-end solution to process distributed data at scale, and implement data governance processes using a collaborative and curated approach for enhancing data literacy and trust. This robust environment makes it possible to scale to any level and support any complex data type, so companies can focus on analyzing information instead of manually integrating data.

About Talend






Talend, a leader in data integration and data integrity, is changing the way the world makes decisions.
Talend Data Fabric is the only platform that seamlessly combines an extensive range of data integration and governance capabilities to actively manage the health of corporate information. This unified approach is unique and essential to delivering complete, clean, and uncompromised data in real-time to all employees. It has made it possible to create innovations like the Talend Trust Score™, an industry-first assessment that instantly quantifies the reliability of any data set. 
Over 6,500 customers across the globe have chosen Talend to run their businesses on healthy data. Talend is recognized as a leader in its field by leading analyst firms and industry media.
