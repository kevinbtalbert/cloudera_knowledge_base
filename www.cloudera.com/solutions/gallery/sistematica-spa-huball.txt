Huball - a unified application hub for car, health and home ecosystems
Huball connects, acquires and analyzes the data collected by fitness bands, smart watches, smart home devices, car black boxes and OBD dongles. It normalizes and stores all the data into a powerful and scalable big data architecture and provides actionable reporting and insights. Huball connects market devices by different vendors (Fitbit, Jawbone, Withings, Netatmo, Wemo, Nest, Garmin, TomTom) and can integrate custom devices specifically developed for vertical applications.







 



 




How it works
Device Connection 

It connects car, health and home ecosystems, using market devices such as fitness bands, smart watches, smart home devices, car GPS black boxes and OBD devices. 
Huball allows the integration of custom devices using Huball SDK and API

Acquisition & Storage

All the data from the three ecosystems are acquired normalized and stored into a flexible and scalable Cloudera platform, using unique data structures regardless of data sources and formats

Information Delivery

Big data architecture for faster data processing and rapid analytics, with actionable reporting and insights. We provide direct access to raw data with the Rest API for your custom apps








 



 




Positive Business Outcomes

Manage all customers, all ecosystems, all devices from different makes and models, into a unique application hub
Exploit and open new business models and solutions in Insurance Telematics combining together and correlating car, health and home ecosystems
Strengthen the customer engagement and retention for Utilities and Telco Operators
Suitable for healthcare service providers to build data collection and analysis applications, using wearable devices on the market
Fast application building for corporate branding and enterprise welfare
Access to a standardized and homogeneous database
Powerful and ready to use analytics and insights








 



 




Differentiators

Tested and ready to use modules, letting the customer to focus on application issues and reducing time to market 
Rest API to access both aggregated and raw data, to develop custom applications and to integrate with Customer’s Enterprise systems
Extensible with coaching systems that provide tips and suggestions to help the end user improve their performance
