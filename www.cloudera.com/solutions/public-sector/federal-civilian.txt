      Better decisions begin with data visibility
      





Federal agencies have an opportunity to transform the way decisions are made by adopting a modern platform for machine learning and analytics, optimized for the cloud. Complete data visibility informs decision-making and can impact everything from tax collection and immigration to infrastructure repairs and disaster recovery.








 







    
    
      Actionable insight for federal agencies
      












Prepare for cyber attacks and forest fires with complete enterprise visibility
Provide a singular view of data to help agencies such as DHS inform first responders and enable citizens to access critical information on services in real time.
DHS partners with Cloudera to improve citizen safety









Improve analytic flexibility — optimize your security information event management (SIEM) software
Dramatically reduce the cost of your current security operations environment while increasing visibility. Retain long-term data and enhance your overall data intelligence.
Learn how to optimize your SIEM with Cloudera
Why more than 50% of SIEM users are evaluating modern platforms for machine learning









 










Improve citizen health with enhanced patient monitoring and accelerated clinical prediction
Employ sensor technology for remote healthcare monitoring, contain the spread of infectious disease, and oversee payments for waste fraud and abuse of government funds.

Children’s Healthcare of Atlanta improves pain management for premature babies
Inova uncovers breakthrough insights to improve patient health with machine learning









Predict maintenance needs and enhance government asset efficiencies
Accurately predict the probability, timing, and cause of equipment failure. Enable early and corrective measures in the most effective way, avoiding unplanned downtime and reducing costs.
Mobilizing the military with data visibility









 










Replace extract, transfer, and load (ETL), and operational data stores (ODS)
Cost effectively address the need for data collection and storage to support the mission of your agency. Replace outdated systems and offload workloads from expensive data warehouses to take advantage of machine learning and analytics.
ODS in government









Build smarter vehicles and enable connected transit
Provide effective and efficient roads and public transit solutions through visibility into sensor data. Improve services and control costs by monitoring everything from vehicle maintenance to social media feedback.

Reduce risk and enhance regulatory compliance
